Admin Instructions

This app handles book submissions and requests in a two step process. Below are the instruction for the book club coordinator to help manage this process.

Step 1: Go to the home screen and click the 'Book Submission Tool'. Select a date for the the submission deadline and click 'Start'. The book club coordinator many then contact the book club members and direct them to the Book Club App main website to login and begin submitting books. Club members will have until the deadline to submit up to 3 books and can adjust their submissions up until the deadline you set.

Step 2: After the submission deadline is passed, the book club coordinator can navigate to the Voting Tool'. The most recent submissions collection will be visible on the screen. Select a deadline for voting in end and click 'Start'. The book club coordinator can notify the club members that voting has commenced. Club members will log back in and must submit their ranked vote before the deadline. Members can edit their votes up until the deadline.

After the voting deadline has passed, the coordinator can go back into the voting tool to see the top 12 books selected by the group.

Have any questions? Feel free to contact as at SFBookClubHelp@SFBC.org.
